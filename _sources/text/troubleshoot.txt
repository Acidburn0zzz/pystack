Pystack let iptables rules in filter table
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

This mean the stack is been shutdown suddently or that as socket has not been closed.
Iptables is a weakness of pystack because it induce this kind of side effects.
